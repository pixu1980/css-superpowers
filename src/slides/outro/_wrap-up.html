Wrapping up

CSS Custom Properties and CSS Layers are fundamental tools for effectively addressing the needs of re-branding, re-selling, and multi-tenant.

Pros

  • Proactive Strategy
  • Efficiency
  • Modularity
  • Maintainability
  • Progressive Enhancement
  • Visual Bug Hunting time reduction

Cons

  • Learning & Practice Curve
  • Bootstrapping projects initially more complex
  • Minimum impact on performances

Conclusions

  • Over-engineering as strategic investment
  • Being prepared to future evolutions of CSS
  • Invite to Action: Experiment & Share
